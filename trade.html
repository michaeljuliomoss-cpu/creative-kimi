Tuesdays and Thursdays

Sell us your books

We buy used books for store credit on Tuesdays and Thursdays. No appointments, no appointments needed. Here is how it works.

  1. Bring your books to the shop on a Tuesday or Thursday, any time between 8 AM and one hour before close. We buy year-round, rain or shine.
  2. Leave them at the counter or fill out a drop-off slip if Jonas is already with another seller. We take a first look while you browse or sit with coffee.
  3. Jonas reviews each title. We look for condition, scarcity, and whether it fits the shelves. If we do not need it, we will tell you straight — no hard feelings, and your books come home with you.
  4. We offer store credit at a third of what we will sell the book for. A clean paperback we would price at $12 gets you $4 in credit. Hardcovers and scarce editions can go higher.
  5. Credit is good forever. No expiration. We keep a paper ledger with your name and balance — no app, no account login. Spend it on anything in the shop.

What we take

We accept

  • Literature and fiction in good condition
  • Poetry, especially out-of-print editions
  • Cookbooks from any decade
  • Works in translation
  • NYRB Classics and similar reprint series
  • Scholarly nonfiction and art monographs

We do not take

  • Textbooks, workbooks, or study guides
  • Mass-market genre paperbacks in poor condition
  • Magazines (except vintage Gourmet or New Yorker)
  • Damaged, musty, or water-stained books
  • Encyclopedias or reference sets from before 2000
  • Anything with underlining or highlighting throughout

Store credit only. We do not buy for cash. Credit is non-transferable and recorded by name. If you prefer to donate, we are happy to pass your books to the Carroll Gardens library sale.
